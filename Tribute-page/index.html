Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011 | Visionary, Innovator, Apple Co-founder

About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, and inventor. He was the co-founder of Apple Inc., a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, and a revolutionary thinker in the tech industry.

Jobs introduced products that changed the world — the iPhone, iPad, iPod, and MacBook, transforming how we interact with technology. His commitment to elegant design, simplicity, and innovation inspired millions across the globe.

Quotes

"Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life."
"Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower."

Legacy

Steve Jobs passed away in 2011, but his impact lives on through Apple's ecosystem and the mindset he fostered in entrepreneurs worldwide.
